## Design Patterns Identified

### Consistent Elements Across Both Designs:
- Purple hero section
- White service cards with icons
- Orange/red gradient CTA buttons
- Carousel/slider functionality for steps, events, testimonials
- Light colored backgrounds for alternating sections
- Dark footer with newsletter signup
- Rounded corners on cards and buttons
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Responsive carousel navigation with dots

### Color Scheme:
- **Primary Purple**: Hero sections, step section backgrounds
- **Orange/Red Gradient**: Primary CTA buttons
- **White**: Cards, backgrounds, text (on dark backgrounds)
- **Dark Blue/Navy**: Footer background
- **Light Peach/Pink**: Alternating section backgrounds
- **Gold/Yellow**: Accent icons for benefits and steps
